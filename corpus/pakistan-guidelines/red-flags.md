# Red flags — seek emergency care (Pakistan)

## Chest pain
Severe chest pain, pain spreading to arm or jaw, sweating, or difficulty breathing may indicate a heart emergency. Go to the nearest hospital emergency immediately or call Rescue 1122 / Edhi 115.

## Stroke signs
Sudden face drooping, arm weakness, slurred speech — treat as emergency. Do not wait.

## Dengue warning signs
Persistent vomiting, severe abdominal pain, bleeding gums, cold/clammy skin after fever — hospital emergency.

## Pregnancy
Heavy vaginal bleeding, severe abdominal pain in pregnancy — emergency department immediately.

## Breathing
Severe shortness of breath, blue lips, or inability to speak in full sentences — emergency.
